> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Learning and Working Environment

The learning environment was professional, friendly, and highly supportive. Throughout the program, mentors and organizers were always willing to help whenever I encountered technical difficulties during laboratory exercises. The open atmosphere encouraged participants to ask questions, exchange ideas, and continuously improve their knowledge.

### 2. Support from Mentors and Program Team

The mentors provided detailed explanations of AWS concepts and practical guidance for every laboratory exercise. Instead of simply providing solutions, they encouraged me to analyze problems independently and develop my own troubleshooting skills. The program team also kept participants well informed by providing learning materials, announcements, and timely support whenever needed.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Major

The program was highly relevant to my Information Technology studies. It enabled me to apply theoretical knowledge in real cloud environments while learning modern technologies that are widely used in the IT industry today.

### 4. Learning and Professional Development

The FCAJ program provided excellent opportunities to develop both technical and soft skills. I gained hands-on experience with AWS services such as IAM, EC2, VPC, Amazon S3, Amazon RDS, AWS Lambda, Amazon ECS, Amazon ECR, Amazon Bedrock, CloudWatch, and AWS Budgets. In addition, I improved my teamwork, communication, time management, problem-solving, and technical documentation skills.

### 5. Team Culture

One of the most impressive aspects of the program was the collaborative culture. Mentors and participants were always willing to share their knowledge and support each other. This positive learning environment motivated me to become more proactive and confident throughout the internship.

### 6. Training Program

The program followed a well-structured learning roadmap that combined theoretical knowledge with extensive hands-on laboratory exercises. The practical labs closely reflected real-world cloud deployment scenarios, allowing participants to gain valuable industry experience.

## Additional Questions

### What did you enjoy the most during the program?

The most rewarding experience was having the opportunity to work directly with AWS cloud services. Completing real laboratory exercises and deploying cloud resources gave me practical experience that significantly enhanced my understanding of cloud computing.

### What improvements would you suggest?

I believe the program could include more team-based projects where participants collaborate to build complete cloud solutions. Additional workshops on DevOps, Kubernetes, Terraform, Infrastructure as Code, and CI/CD would also be valuable.

### Would you recommend this program to others?

Absolutely. I would strongly recommend the FCAJ program to students who are interested in cloud computing because it offers a well-designed learning path, experienced mentors, practical AWS laboratories, and an excellent environment for professional development.

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

- Provide more project-based laboratory exercises.
- Organize additional workshops with AWS experts and industry professionals.
- Include more advanced topics such as DevOps, AI/ML, cloud security, and Infrastructure as Code.
- Continuously update the learning content to keep pace with new AWS technologies.
- I hope to participate in future FCAJ programs or contribute as a mentor after gaining more industry experience.
